Empanadas are a delightful Latin American treat, consisting of flaky pastry pockets filled with a variety of savory or sweet fillings. The most common savory version features seasoned ground beef, diced onions, and olives, while a pinch of raisins adds a hint of sweetness. These golden-brown parcels are baked or fried to perfection, creating a satisfying contrast of crispy outer layers and flavorful interiors.

Empanadas are a versatile snack or meal option, offering endless possibilities for creativity with fillings such as cheese, chicken, vegetables, or even dessert versions filled with dulce de leche. With their portability and delicious fillings, empanadas are a cherished treat that embodies the culinary diversity of Latin America.
