Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian classic, featuring layers of pasta sheets enveloping a savory filling of ground beef, sausage, ricotta, and melted mozzarella. A homemade tomato sauce adds a burst of flavor to this comforting dish, which emerges from the oven golden and bubbling, ready to delight the senses with its delicious layers.

This iconic lasagna can be customized with ingredients like spinach, mushrooms, or seafood, making it a versatile choice for family dinners or gatherings with friends. With its enticing blend of flavors and textures, lasagna captures the essence of Italian cuisine, leaving a lasting impression on all who savor it.

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat
  • Sauce

  2. Prepare meat sauce
