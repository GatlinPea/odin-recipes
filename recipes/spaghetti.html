Spaghetti

Ingredients

Spaghetti is a beloved Italian classic that celebrates simplicity and flavor in every bite. It starts with al dente pasta, typically made from durum wheat semolina, cooked to perfection and then tossed in a luscious sauce. The most iconic version features a rich tomato sauce simmered with garlic, onions, basil, and a touch of olive oil.

Grated Parmesan or Pecorino Romano cheese is generously sprinkled over the top, adding a delightful umami kick. Spaghetti's versatility allows for endless variations, including creamy Alfredo sauce, zesty marinara, or hearty meatballs. This beloved Italian staple is not just a meal but a culinary journey that brings comfort and satisfaction to pasta lovers around the world.
